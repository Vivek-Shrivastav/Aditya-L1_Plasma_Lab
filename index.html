Mission Payloads

Aditya-L1 carries advanced in-situ particle and magnetic field suites to decode the solar wind's mysteries.

ASPEX / SWIS

The Solar Wind Ion Spectrometer measures the energy and angular distribution of the solar wind ions, primarily protons and alphas.

ASPEX / STEPS

Supra-Thermal and Energetic Particle Spectrometer tracks high-energy particles to study solar flares and acceleration mechanisms.

PAPA Suite

Plasma Analyser Package for Aditya studies the composition of solar wind and its distribution across various energy levels.

Magnetometer

Measures the magnitude and nature of the Interplanetary Magnetic Field (IMF) at the Sun-Earth L1 point.
