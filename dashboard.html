Magnetic PSD — Power Spectral Density
X: Frequency f [Hz] log scale · Y: PSD [(nT)² Hz⁻¹] log scale · Orange dashed: Kolmogorov −5/3 · Purple dashed: IK −3/2 · Method: Welch averaged periodogram (Paschmann & Daly Ch. 1)
The PSD reveals the turbulent energy cascade in the solar wind. The Kolmogorov −5/3 law applies to the MHD inertial range. Below the ion cyclotron frequency (f_ci), the spectrum steepens to −7/3 or −4 as kinetic Alfvén waves dominate. Energy injection from a CME or shock shallows the spectrum. A spectral break marks the transition from MHD to kinetic scales.
